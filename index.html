NIH Data Management & Sharing Plan Information

These pages provide resources compiled by the Alaska INBRE Data Science Core to assist researchers in creating a comprehensive Data Management and Sharing (DMS) Plan. This guidance follows the rules outlined in the NIH Data Management and Sharing Policy (NOT-OD-21-013).

This page provides general guidelines and policies and a checklist identifying the six core elements of DMS plans as required by the NIH. In addition, we include a number of example DMS plans and a page with frequently asked questions. Each section includes direct links to relevant NIH guidelines and resources for further information.

Note for researchers in Alaska

If your research involves working with American Indian/Alaska Native participants, please check the NIH guidelines. For additional information and questions about working with Alaskan communities, please contact the Alaska INBRE Community Based Participatory Research Core.
